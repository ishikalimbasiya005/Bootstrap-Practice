Bootstrap Margin-Padding:----

    m: Stands for margin.
    p: Stands for padding.

            {t|b|l|r|x|y}: Specifies the side:--
    t: Top
    b: Bottom
    l: Left
    r: Right
    x: Horizontal (left and right)
    y: Vertical (top and bottom)

    {size}: Spacing size:
            0: 0px
            1: 0.25rem
            2: 0.5rem
            3: 1rem
            4: 1.5rem
            5: 3rem
            auto: Automatically adjusts margin & Padding(useful for centering).

Bootstrap Margin and Padding Example

Box 1:
Margin: m-3 (1rem)
Padding: p-3 (1rem)
Box 2:
Margin Top: mt-4 (1.5rem)
Padding Start (left): ps-5 (3rem)
Box 3:
Margin Y-axis: my-3 (1rem top & bottom)
Padding X-axis: px-4 (1.5rem left & right)
Box 4 (Centered):
Margin X-axis: mx-auto (auto for centering)
Padding: p-2 (0.5rem)
